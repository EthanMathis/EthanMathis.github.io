Projects

Headspace

Headspace is a songwriting tool for musicians. It offers a place for songwriters to store all of their musical creations in one convenient place. Headspace also enables musicians to collaborate on songs together by granting edit rights to their friends. Finally, users can even record themselves & upload their sound files to Headspace via Firebase.

Check out Headspace

Nutshell

Nutshell is a social hub for users to track their daily tasks, local events, news articles & make friends. It also offers public and private message boards and even allows you to check the weather forecast for events. This was my first React application and one of the larger group projects I worked on while learning front-end development at NSS.

Check out Nutshell

Holiday Road

A team project where my team and I built an application to allow users to build itineraries and plan trips to any of the national parks maintained by the National Parks Service. The app starts you off in Nashville, TN and allows the user to build an itinerary by selecting options from a list of national parks, restaurants and fun attractions and save it for reference.

Check out Holiday Road
